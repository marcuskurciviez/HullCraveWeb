About Us

Welcome to Crave, your local family-owned smoothie shop right here in Hull, MA. At Crave, we're all about fresh. Fresh ingredients, fresh tastes, and a fresh approach to feeding our community.
Our menu is simple but full of variety. We serve a delicious range of smoothies, bowls packed with nutrients, homemade baked goods for your sweet tooth, and a selection of coffee and iced coffee to kick-start your day. Every item we serve is prepared with care and the freshest ingredients we can find because we believe that good food is at the heart of good living.
Whether you're looking for a quick snack or a refreshing drink, we've got something to satisfy your cravings. Come on in and taste the difference fresh makes!
